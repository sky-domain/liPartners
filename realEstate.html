Real Estate

Li & Partners provides comprehensive legal services in all aspects of real estate law. Our firm has extensive experience representing individual homeowners, investors, and developers in a wide range of real estate transactions, including residential properties, commercial properties, and land development projects.

Our transactional services cover property purchases and sales, mortgage financing, refinancing, title transfers, and related registrations, ensuring that each transaction is conducted efficiently, securely, and in full compliance with legal requirements.

In the area of commercial real estate, our practice encompasses various types of properties such as gas stations, office buildings, golf courses, large-scale warehouses, and farms. We assist clients with every stage of the transaction, including due diligence, purchase negotiations, financing arrangements, and closing documentation.

We also provide specialized legal support for Plaza and commercial land development projects, including project structuring, development and construction agreements, financing documentation, and condominium or multi-unit registrations. Our goal is to help clients successfully manage and complete their development projects from planning to closing with confidence and clarity.
