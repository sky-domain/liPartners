About Us

Li Law Professional Corporation (Li & Partners Law Firm) was founded at the end of 2009. Our legal name is Li Law Professional Corporation. Following close associations with other lawyers, Li Law Professional Corporation applied for and began using the trade name Li and Partners. Benefiting from Mr. Li's professional background and his practice experience in both China and Canada, the firm has enjoyed stable and rapid growth since its inception. Li & Partners Law Firm handles a significant proportion of residential and commercial real estate transactions within the Greater Toronto Area's Chinese community. According to statistics from First Canadian Title ("FCT"), Canada's largest title insurance company, the transaction volume of Li & Partners Law Firm consistently ranked among the top 25% of FCT's volume across all of Canada from 2014 until prior to the pandemic (as noted in Firm Updates ). In addition to residential real estate, Li & Partners Law Firm is seeing strong growth in commercial real estate projects and corporate investment and financing. Mr. Li currently assists investors in Toronto with the acquisition of major commercial real estate projects, including gas stations, office buildings, golf courses, large warehouses, and plaza developments. Furthermore, Mr. Li provides legal services for the expansion of the famous franchised overseas brand “machi machi” in Canada.

Li, Shu Kuan

Barrister and Solicitor Notary Public

Mr. Li, Shu Kuan founded "Li Law Professional Corporation" (currently, "Li & Partners Law Firm") at the end of 2009. Mr. Li earned his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) from the Law School of Renmin University of China and later obtained his Juris Doctor (J.D.) degree from the Faculty of Law at the University of Windsor in Canada. He previously worked at a Provincial High People's Court in China, DLA Piper LLP (a world-class law firm), and a boutique law firm specializing in private equity and corporate finance located on Toronto's Financial District.
