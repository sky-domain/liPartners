Corporate Finance & General Corporate Law

Li & Partners provides comprehensive corporate law and corporate finance legal services, assisting clients through every stage—from company formation to complex mergers, acquisitions, and financing transactions.

Our corporate and finance practice includes:

  • Incorporation and Corporate Governance – Assisting clients with business incorporation, preparing corporate minute books, and drafting shareholders’ agreements.
  • Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) – Providing legal advice and opinions on share purchase agreements, share subscription agreements, and related transactional documentation.
  • Startup and Financing Advisory – Supporting early-stage and growing businesses in raising funds through angel investors, venture capital, and private equity financing, and providing legal opinions on term sheets, share purchase agreements (SPA), investors’ rights agreements, and SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) documents.
  • Founder and Investor Consultation – Advising founders and investors on critical legal issues such as anti-dilution provisions, investor representation on the board of directors, voting structures, and corporate governance matters.
