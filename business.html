Business Purchase & Sale

At Li & Partners, we provide legal services for clients involved in the purchase or sale of established businesses, including restaurants, convenience stores, cafés, dry cleaners, bars, and bubble tea shops.

Our firm assists clients by reviewing transaction structures and key documents to ensure that every business transfer is completed smoothly, securely, and in compliance with applicable laws.

Our legal services include:

  • Reviewing and drafting business purchase and sale agreements
  • Reviewing and negotiating commercial leases
  • Examining and advising on franchise or licensing agreements
  • Assisting with due diligence, asset transfers, business licences, and tax registration updates
